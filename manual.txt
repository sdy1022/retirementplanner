ROTH CONVERSION TRANCHE PLANNER — USER MANUAL
File: roth_tranche_planner.html
====================================================================

WHAT IT DOES
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Splits your annual Roth conversion into three scheduled tranches
sized to fill the 22% federal bracket (MFJ) without spilling into
24%, and flags an optional "bonus" tranche when QQQ/VOO is down 10%
or more from its 52-week high (converting while depressed moves more
shares per tax dollar).

It is a single self-contained HTML file. Open it in any browser --
no server, no install. Your inputs are saved in the browser
(localStorage), so they are still there the next time you open it
on the same machine and browser.

THE ANNUAL RHYTHM: RUN IT THREE TIMES A YEAR
--------------------------------------------------------------------
The tool is designed around three check-ins. At each one you update
a few fields, set the checkpoint dropdown, and read off the tranche
amounts.

  January  -> execute Tranche I
  October  -> execute Tranche II
  Mid-Dec  -> execute Tranche III

--------------------------------------------------------------------
CHECK-IN 1: JANUARY
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Section 01 -- fill in for the new tax year:
   - 22% Bracket Ceiling: top of the MFJ 22% bracket (taxable
     income). UPDATE THIS EVERY YEAR as the IRS adjusts brackets.
   - Other Taxable Income: full-year salary + other known W-2
     income, EXCLUDING bonus, with deductions already netted out
     (i.e., this is taxable income, not gross).
   - Bonus / Variable Comp Estimate: your best January guess.
   - Target Annual Conversion: how much you want to convert this
     year in total.

2. Section 02 -- leave "Projected Full-Year Taxable Income" and
   "Conversions Executed YTD" at 0. Set the checkpoint dropdown to
   "January -- Tranche 1 only".

3. Read the Tranche Schedule:
   - Execute Tranche I now. It is sized off salary alone (bonus
     ignored), so it is safe even in a zero-bonus year.
   - Tranche II and III amounts shown in January are PREVIEWS only.
     Do not execute them yet -- they get re-computed in the fall.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
CHECK-IN 2: OCTOBER
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Section 02:
   - Projected Full-Year Taxable Income: your updated estimate of
     the FULL YEAR (through Dec 31), now that the bonus is mostly
     visible. Salary + confirmed bonus, EXCLUDING any Roth
     conversions. Do NOT enter a literal year-to-date paystub
     figure -- project it to year end.
   - Conversions Executed YTD: what you actually converted in
     Tranche I. (Leave 0 to assume it went exactly as planned.)
   - Checkpoint: "October -- Tranche 2 true-up".

2. Execute Tranche II at the amount shown.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
CHECK-IN 3: MID-DECEMBER
--------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Section 02:
   - Projected Full-Year Taxable Income: final number -- at this
     point it should be nearly exact.
   - Conversions Executed YTD: total of Tranche I + Tranche II as
     actually executed.
   - Checkpoint: "December -- Tranche 3 final".

2. Execute Tranche III. It closes the remaining bracket room with a
   $200 safety buffer so a small estimate error doesn't tip you
   into the 24% bracket.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
SECTION 03: DRAWDOWN CHECK (ANY TIME)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Check this whenever the market drops -- it is independent of the
Jan/Oct/Dec schedule.

   - 52-Week High and Current Price: for QQQ or VOO, per share.
   - Max Bonus Conversion: the largest EXTRA conversion you would
     do on a dip. Keep roughly 25% of this amount available in
     cash in your taxable tax-source pool to pay the tax on it.

If the price is >= 10% below the 52-week high, a green "Bonus"
tranche appears. It is optional and opportunistic. NOTE: the bonus
tranche is taxable income like any other conversion -- the tool
includes it in the over-ceiling check, so watch for the warning
before executing both the bonus and Tranche III.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
READING THE OUTPUT
--------------------------------------------------------------------
Callout box (above the gauge):
   - Red "Over ceiling" -- planned conversions + projected income
     exceed the 22% bracket top by the amount shown. Trim Tranche
     III (or skip the bonus tranche) before executing.
   - "Drawdown trigger active / No drawdown trigger" -- status of
     the 10% dip rule.

Bracket Gauge:
   - Bar segments left to right: ordinary income, T1, T2, T3, and
     Bonus (green) if active. The red vertical line marked
     "22%->24%" is the bracket ceiling -- nothing should cross it.

Tranche Schedule statuses:
   - OK    -- sized from current, confirmed numbers; safe to act on.
   - WATCH -- a preview based on estimates; will be re-computed at a
             later checkpoint.
   - OVER  -- this tranche would breach the ceiling; reduce it.

Footline: remaining bracket room right now, plus the sizing rules.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW THE TRANCHES ARE SIZED (THE RULES)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
   Tranche I   = min(60% of target, 90% of salary-only room)
   Tranche II  = January preview: 65% of the remainder, capped at
                 90% of the room left after T1 (bonus estimate
                 included). October: re-sized against your updated
                 full-year projection.
   Tranche III = whatever is left of the target, capped at the
                 remaining room minus a $200 buffer.
   Room math keeps a 10% safety margin throughout.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
EXECUTION RULES (per the plan this tool implements)
--------------------------------------------------------------------
   - All tranches converted IN-KIND (transfer shares, don't sell).
   - Zero tax withholding on the conversion itself.
   - Pay the tax from the taxable brokerage tax-source pool using
     specific lot ID.

--------------------------------------------------------------------
LIMITATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------
   - Federal bracket only. Indiana's flat state tax applies on top
     and is NOT modeled.
   - Assumes MFJ filing status.
   - Does not model IRMAA cliffs, NIIT, or capital-gains bracket
     interactions -- if you are near an IRMAA threshold, check that
     separately before executing.
   - Inputs persist per browser/machine. Clearing browser data
     resets them to the built-in defaults.
